What is CSS?

  • CSS or Cascading Style Sheet, is a styling language that paints a website with colors, fonts, layouts, and animations.
  • CSS is used to create stunning webpages. It can enhance the user experience, and can make a webpage unqiue and stand out amongst the rest. It is beautiful to see your webpage come to life and become even more beautiful.
